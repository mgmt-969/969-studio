About
the
Studio

Suanni hosted the first 969 event at Mr. Mahjongs alone and honestly, it was really hard. Since then we've come a long way from that first night to throwing Ashes of Eden renegade, and hosting some of the largest techno artists from the EU.

We're thankful to see our community continue to grow. To bring together amazing talent, the most beautiful people and energy, and ultimately create a safe space where people can truly be themselves and find a way to connect with others.

Thank you to everyone who has supported this vision of what could be. Forever thankful and grateful.
